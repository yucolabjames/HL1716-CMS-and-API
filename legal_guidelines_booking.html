參觀申請須知

  1. 可持續資源館參觀活動只接受團體預約申請。逢星期二至五 (公眾假期除外)，每節參觀時段人數最多40人；星期六、日及公眾假期每節參觀時段最多30人。每節約2小時 (包括參觀可持續資源館及工作坊)。
  2. 展館開放時間為逢星期二至五上午九時半至下午五時，星期六日及公眾假期上午九時半至下午六時，逢星期一休息(公眾假期除外)。
  3. 由於導賞團名額有限，申請將以先到先得形式辦理。團體可於參觀日三個月內及不少於7天前提交申請。本館會於收到申請表後七天內以電郵方式通知申請人申請的結果，並附上預約編號。
  4. 申請者如沒有填寫足夠、正確或清楚的資料，或會影響申請處理時間和結果。
  5. 預約一經接納後不能轉讓。
  6. 團體如欲取消申請，請於參觀日前不少於3個工作天以電郵通知本館，逾期取消預約將視作缺席處理。
  7. 團體必須根據約定時間 15 分鐘前到達本館登記處，並出示確認電郵/預約編號以確認身分。團體如遲到超過30分鐘或以上，本館有權取消遲到團體的導賞服務。
  8. 團體如缺席或到訪人數少於一半，該團體之預約權利將於到訪/缺席當日起計暫停兩個月。
  9. 如團體安排長者、兒童或傷健人士到訪，負責人須安排足夠人手照顧同行人士。如團體安排有特殊需要人士到訪，請先聯絡本館以作安排。
  10. 預約團體可使用公共交通工具前往資源館，如自行安排交通，到訪前必須以電郵提供旅遊巴士車牌號碼作登記，未有登記的車輛不得進入。
  11. 如個人資料、預約資料或車牌有誤或更改，請盡快致電 3184 2384 或電郵至 iba@hkbu.edu.hk 更新。
  12. 如天文台於早上9時前發出黑色暴雨警告信號或8號或以上的熱帶氣旋警告，本館將全日不開放，並不會向受惡劣天氣所影響的參觀人士作另行通知。受影響的團體可於原定參觀時間的4個月內重新安排參觀。如在開放期間天文台預告將於數小時後發出黑色暴雨警告信號或8號或以上的熱帶氣旋警告，本館將會暫停開放並安排參加者盡快離開。
  13. 本館擁有最終決定權。如申請者違反以上規定或提供虛假資料，本館有權取消已批准的申請。

Guidelines for Visit Booking

Welcome to the “S Gallery”! Please observe and comply with the following house rules:

  1. Only group booking is accepted for S Gallery visits.
    • Tuesdays to Fridays (except Public Holidays) : Maximum 40 persons for each session
    • Saturdays, Sundays and Public Holidays: Maximum 30 persons for each session.
    Each session is 2 hours (include S Gallery visit and workshop)
  2. The opening hour of the Gallery is Tuesdays to Fridays 9:30am to 5pm, Saturdays to Sundays and public holidays 9:30am to 6pm, closed on Mondays (except public holidays).
  3. Due to limited quota for guided tours, application is processed on a first-come-first-served basis. Applications can be made up to three (3) months in advance and must not be less than seven (7) days before the proposed date of visit. The Gallery will notify the applicant the result of the application through email with booking reference number within 7 days from the date of receiving the application form.
  4. Any incomplete, insufficient or incorrect data may affect the processing time and the outcome of the application.
  5. No transferable of the approved application. Cancellation of booking should be made 3 days prior to the date of visit by email toS Gallery. Late cancellation will be regarded as absence.
  6. Visiting groups should arrive 15 minutes before the confirmed time of visit and show the confirmation email/booking result number at registration counter. The Gallery reserves the right to cancel the guided tour service of late comers if the visiting groups late for 30 min or above.
  7. Groups not showing up or less than 50% of the total no. of persons will have their booking right being suspended for 2 months after the original scheduled date.
  8. For elderly, children or persons with disability, they should be taken care by their visiting group. For a group of persons with special needs, please contact S Gallery for prior arrangement.
  9. Visiting groups should arrange their own transportation. Coach plate number shall be provided for registration prior to the visit. Vehicles without prior registration are not allowed to enter S Gallery.
  10. If there is any changes of contact person, booking details or coach plate number need to be amended, please call 3184 2384 or email to iba@hkbu.edu.hk for amendment.
  11. If Black Rainstorm Warning is issued or Typhoon Signal No. 8 or above is hoisted before 9am, S Gallery will be closed for whole day. For visits that are disrupted by inclement weather, the respective visitors will not be informed separately. Affected group can reschedule the visit date within 4 months after the original date of visit. If Black Rainstorm warning or Typhoon Signal No. 8 or above is expected to be issued/hoisted within a few hours, visitors will be notified of the pending closure of S Gallery and arranged to leave as soon as possible.
  12. The decision of S Gallery shall be final for the approval of application. If the applicant violates the above provisions or provides false information, S Gallery reserves the right to cancel any approved application.
