參觀守則

歡迎來到「可持續資源館」！敬請注意及遵守以下場地守則:

  1. 可持續資源館開放時間:
    • 星期二至五 (09:30-17:00)
    • 星期六日及公眾假期 (09:30-18:00)
    • 星期一休息
  2. 星期二至五每時段最多參觀人數為40人，星期六日及公眾假期每時段最多30人。
  3. 如當日參觀人數太多，本館將因應展覽館可容納人數，按先到先得方法安排參觀者／使用者入場。
  4. 未經本館職員許可，參觀者／使用者不得在館內展示橫額、彩旗、宣傳單張或任何形式之廣告。
  5. 未經許可，不得在館內進行錄影、錄音，或使用任何複製器材。
  6. 未經本館職員許可，參觀者／使用者不得在館內派發推廣資料、紀念品及贈品。
  7. 參觀者／使用者不得擅自處理、擾亂或以其他方式干預任何展品。
  8. 參觀者／使用者不得在館內進食、吸煙、奔跑或喧嘩。
  9. 參觀者／使用者不得在館內拋擲物件、粗言穢語或行為不檢。
  10. 請勿帶同寵物到訪 (陪同視障人士之導盲犬除外) 。
  11. 參觀者／使用者不得故意在展覽館內的任何牆壁、門戶、家具、裝置、其他物件或任何展品上塗寫或劃上記號，或將其弄污、銷毀或以任何其他方式損壞。倘因違反本守則而造成本館的家具、裝置、其他物件或任何展品遺失、損毀，或招致本館負擔額外的開支，參觀者／使用者均須作出賠償。參觀者／使用者須繳付的數額相當於展覽館負責人認為足以重新添置或維修該項展品所需的款項。
  12. 本館為保安電視監察及攝錄地帶。
  13. 參觀者／使用者不得故意妨礙任何正在執行職責的職員，或故意妨礙、騷擾、干擾或煩擾任何其他正在使用本館或其內所提供的設施的人。
  14. 參觀者／使用者不得向本館借取任何展品。
  15. 參觀者／使用者不得進入館內任何不向公眾開放的部分。
  16. 請小心保管隨身攜帶之物件，如有任何遺失或損壞，本館概不負責。
  17. 本館職員有權要求沒有適當及正確使用本館設施、展品和資料，或滋擾其他參觀者／使用者的人士停止有關活動或離開展館。
  18. 本館保留權利及絕對酌情權隨時改變、更改、增補、刪除、修訂及更新此場地守則而無須給予任何理由及預先通知。

General Guidelines of Visit

Welcome to the “S Gallery”! Please observe and comply with the following house rules:

  1. S Gallery Opening Hours:
    • Tuesdays to Fridays (09:30 – 17:00)
    • Saturdays, Sundays and Public Holidays (09:30-18:00)
    • Closed on Mondays.
  2. Maximum 40 persons for each session from Tuesdays to Fridays and maximum 30 persons for each session on Saturdays, Sundays and Public Holidays.
  3. If there are too many visitors at the Gallery, we will arrange the admission on a first come, first served basis according to the capacity of the Gallery.
  4. No banners, buntings, flyers or publicity materials of any kind shall be displayed in the Gallery without the permission of Gallery staff.
  5. No unauthorized video taking, audio tape recording, or use of any reproduction appliances is allowed within the Gallery.
  6. No promotional materials, souvenirs and give-away items shall be distributed in the Gallery without the permission of Gallery staff.
  7. No person shall handle, disturb or otherwise interfere with any exhibit.
  8. Eating, smoking, running or yelling in the Gallery is not allowed.
  9. Throwing objects, employing foul language and behaving in a disorderly manner in the Gallery are prohibited..
  10. No pet should be brought into the Gallery (except trained functional dogs).
  11. No person shall willfully write upon, mark, soil or otherwise damage in any way whatever or destroy any wall, door, furniture, fitting or other thing in the Gallery, or any exhibit. Visitors/users shall indemnify the Gallery against any losses, damages or expenses incurred to the Gallery arising from a breach of these guidelines. A charge will be made for any Gallery exhibit lost or damaged, which will be such sum as the Gallery staff considers necessary to replace the exhibit or set of exhibits which the Gallery exhibit forms a part.
  12. This Gallery is under security camera surveillance and recording.
  13. No person shall willfully obstruct any member of the staff in performing his duty or willfully obstruct, disturb, interrupt or annoy any other person in using the Gallery or of any of the facilities provided therein.
  14. No person shall borrow any exhibit from the Gallery.
  15. No person shall enter any part of the Gallery which is not open for the public.
  16. Please do not leave your belongings unattended. The Gallery is not responsible for the safe custody and/or loss of personal property brought to and/or left in the Gallery.
  17. The Gallery staff reserves the right to ask any person not using the Gallery facilities, exhibits or materials properly and correctly to stop their activities or leave the Gallery.
  18. This Gallery reserves the right and is entitled to change, alter, add, delete, suspend, revise and update all information on this “House Rules” at any time at its absolute discretion without giving any reasons and prior notice.
